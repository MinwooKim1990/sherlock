## 🚨 ALLERGY STATEMENT

**子供がそばアレルギーです。**  
*(Kodomo ga soba allergyii desu.)*

**"My child has a soba allergy."**

---

## FOR HOTEL STAFF

**Child's Name:** Yujin (4 years old)  
**Severe Allergy:** Soba (buckwheat noodles)

**What to do:**
- Do NOT recommend soba noodle dishes
- When suggesting restaurants, ask if they serve soba on the same equipment
- Udon, tempura, gyudon, karaage, and sushi are safe
- If unsure about a dish, ask the restaurant directly

**Safe options:** Udon noodles, grilled chicken, fried foods, rice bowls, sushi

---

## ASKING ABOUT SOBA

**Does this dish have soba?**

**このメニューにそばが入っていますか？**  
*(Kono menu ni soba ga haitte imasu ka?)*

---

**Or point and ask:** 

**"これはそば入ってますか？"**  
*(Kore wa soba haitte masu ka?)*

**"Does this have soba in it?"**

---

## CROSS-CONTAMINATION CHECK

**Was it made on the same cooking surface as soba?**

**そば粉と同じ調理台で作られていませんか？**  
*(Soba-ko to onaji chori-dai de tsukurareteimasu ka?)*

---

## SAFE FOODS FOR YUJIN ✓

- **Udon** — thick wheat noodles (safe)
- **Tempura** — fried vegetables/shrimp (ask about sauce)
- **Gyudon** — beef over rice (safe)
- **Karaage** — fried chicken (safe)
- **Okonomiyaki** — savory pancake (ask about sauce)
- **Conveyor sushi** — raw fish + rice (safe)
- **Yakitori** — grilled chicken skewers (safe)
- **Edamame** — steamed soybeans (safe)
